Load Roll Paper — Epson SureColor P6000

Six actions to remember

Keep the sequence simple and complete the quality check before resuming output.

  1. 1
    Open

    Open the roll-paper cover.

  2. 2
    Remove

    Release and remove the current roll.

  3. 3
    Prepare

    Confirm the new roll and adapters.

  4. 4
    Seat

    Align and lock the new roll.

  5. 5
    Feed

    Feed the leading edge straight.

  6. 6
    Verify

    Confirm settings and print readiness.

!

Never force the equipment. Stop and ask a trained colleague or shift leader if the roll will not seat, the paper will not feed, or an error remains.

Before resuming output

Quick quality check

  • Roll and adapters are secure.
  • Paper feeds straight without slack.
  • Media settings match the installed roll.
  • Automatic cutting is correct.

Training standard

Fast, clear, and consistent

Store leadership should verify approved media settings and local escalation steps before final rollout.
